General Resort FAQs

Q: Where is the resort located and how do we get there?

A: Our resort is located in Bingag, Dauis, Panglao, Bohol, Philippines. Guests can reach us by private car, shuttle service, or public transportation. Detailed directions are provided upon booking.

Q: What are your operating hours?

A: The resort is open daily from Monday to Sunday. Overnight guests have 24-hour access to designated areas.

Q: Do we need to book in advance?

A: Yes, advance reservation is highly recommended, especially on weekends and holidays.

Q: Do you accept walk-ins?

A: Walk-ins are accepted depending on slot availability.

Q: What are your payment options?

A: We accept cash, bank transfer, and selected online payment methods. A down payment may be required for reservations.

Activities & Experiences

Q: Do you offer guided activities or tours?

A: Yes, we offer guided activities led by trained staff to ensure safety and enjoyment.

Q: Are aquatic or water activities available?

A: Yes, guests can enjoy swimming and selected water activities depending on season and availability.

Q: Are safety equipment and staff provided?

A: Yes, safety gear is provided when required, and trained staff are present during activities.

Q: Are children allowed in activities?

A: Yes, children are welcome but must always be supervised by a parent or guardian.

Camping & Outdoor Stay

Q: Do you have camping areas?

A: Yes, we offer designated camping areas with safe and scenic surroundings.

Q: Are tents and equipment provided?

A: Guests may bring their own equipment or rent available camping gear from the resort.

Q: Is overnight camping allowed?

A: Yes, overnight camping packages are available upon reservation.

Reservation & Policies

Q: How do we book a reservation?

A: You may book through our official page, contact number, or email: info@ecologyresort.com.

Q: Do you offer group or corporate packages?

A: Yes, we offer special packages for group bookings, school trips, and corporate events.

Q: What is your cancellation policy?

A: Cancellations must be made at least a few days before the scheduled stay. Fees may apply depending on the policy.

Q: What safety protocols do you follow?

A: We follow strict safety guidelines, regular equipment checks, and emergency preparedness procedures.
