About the Resort

First established in 1998 as Panglao Island and Nature Resort, our 80-hectare haven has welcomed both local and international guests with warmth and care. Embraced by a half-kilometer stretch of pristine white sand, it is one of the few resorts on the island that can offer such natural beauty and a high level of privacy.

After two decades of pampering thousands of visitors, Panglao Island and Nature Resort proudly rebranded as Ecology Resort in 2016, continuing its tradition of relaxation, comfort, and connection with nature.

Discover a whole new world of experiences at Ecology Resort — your paradise in Bohol.

About What the Place Is

Ecology Resort is an eco-friendly retreat built to bring people closer to nature while enjoying comfort and relaxation. It is a place where guests can rest in spacious villas, walk through green landscapes, white sand beach, and enjoy activities that highlight the beauty of Panglao. More than just a vacation spot, the resort promotes care for the environment and offers a safe, welcoming space for families, couples, and travelers from around the world.
